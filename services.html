Our Services

Hiru Super offers a wide range of services to make your shopping experience convenient and enjoyable.

  • Home Delivery Service
    Order online or by phone and get your groceries delivered to your doorstep. Free delivery for orders over Rs. 5,000.
  • Fresh Produce Daily
    We receive fresh vegetables, fruits, and other produce daily from local farmers, ensuring maximum freshness.
  • Loyalty Program
    Join our loyalty program and earn points on every purchase. Redeem points for discounts and special offers.
  • Online Shopping
    Shop from the comfort of your home through our user-friendly online platform. Browse thousands of products.
  • Bulk Orders
    Special pricing for bulk orders for parties, events, or businesses. Contact us for customized quotes.
  • Bakery & Deli
    Fresh bread baked daily, custom cakes for special occasions, and a variety of deli items.
  • Personal Shopping Assistant
    Need help finding products? Our staff is ready to assist you throughout your shopping journey.
