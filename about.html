About Hiru Super

Our Story

Established in 2010, Hiru Super has been serving the community with fresh and quality products for over a decade. We started as a small grocery store and have grown into one of the most trusted supermarket chains in the region.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the freshest products at the most competitive prices while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We never compromise on product quality
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness is our priority
  • Community Support: We support local farmers and suppliers
  • Sustainability: Committed to eco-friendly practices
